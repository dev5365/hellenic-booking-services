Weekend Getaways For A Relaxing Trip

Beautiful Places To Visit With Your Family For A relaxing Weekend.


NEEMRANA

Known for the famous Neemrana Fort Palace, Neemrana used to be the seat of power of the Chauhan Rajputs. It has recently been developed as a heritage getaway.


LONAVALA

Lonavala and Khandala are home to dramatic landscapes, historical forts, ancient caves, charming sunset points, beautiful lakes and cascading waterfalls.


JAIPUR

Fondly known as the ‘Pink City’ and now declared as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Jaipur’s historic forts, majestic palaces and ancient temples make it a perfect royal getaway in the heart of Aravallis.





GWALIOR

Gwalior is a timeless treasure of art and music. The cityscape is adorned with historical monuments and a hilltop fort that are souvenirs of the dynasties that ruled here.


KASAULI

A quintessential hill station surrounded by pine and oak forests with scenic viewpoints and colonial-era landmarks, Kasauli stands at an altitude of 1,800 metres.


KABINI

Once a famous hunting ground under the British raj, Kabini boasts a diverse flora and fauna. Nagarhole National Park is the star attraction here, providing refuge to many endangered species.

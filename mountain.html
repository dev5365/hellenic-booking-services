Hill Stations To Visit For A Rejuvenating Trip In Nature's Lap

Hill Stations - Breathe in the fresh mountain air, treat your eyes to the spectacular views and lose yourself in the charming ambience to feel refreshed from within.Exploring the Nature!


KASOL

The hill-hamlet of Kasol stands at over 1,580 meters above sea level, in Himachal Pradesh, and flaunts raw natural beauty with lush conifer forests, snow-capped Himalayan mountains and winding rivers.


DALHOUSIE

Located in the lush green surroundings of Chamba district in Himachal Pradesh, Dalhousie is a serene town that still has its natural beauty well preserved. A Beautiful Place To Visit In Himachal Pradesh.


SHIMLA

The erstwhile British ‘Summer Capital’ is packed with colonial-era architectural marvels, picturesque hilly hotspots, and a toy train tagged as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.





LEH

Located at a height of 11,500 feet, Leh finds place in the bucket list of many travel enthusiasts. It will leave you in awe with its iconic monasteries and picturesque landscapes.


KASAULI

A favourite with the filmmakers for its picturesque setting against the backdrop of Lidder River, Pahalgam’s lush valleys, colourful meadows and snow-kissed mountains.


GULMARG

Gulmarg is the crowning glory of Jammu & Kashmir and is located 2,650 meters above sea level. Known as the “Meadow of Flowers”, it boasts of the world’s highest golf-course.
